Why Retasify?

Retasify makes it easy to rent, share, and upcycle clothing — from modern styles to Filipiniana pieces — while celebrating local craftsmanship and reducing fashion waste. Join us in building a more sustainable, circular fashion future.

How Retasify Works

01 Browse & Book

Explore our curated selection of stylish garments. Choose your item, select rental dates, and book it with ease.

02 Choose Drop-off

Pick a nearby location that works for you — from partner hubs to community spaces. Convenient, local, and easy to access.

03 Wear & Return

Enjoy your look for events or everyday moments. Then simply drop it off — we’ll take care of the rest.

04 List & Earn

List your garments, set your rental terms, and start earning — while keeping fashion in motion.

For Rentees

Fashion, Without the Commitment

  • Access Stylish, Quality Wear

    Discover a wardrobe that's fresh, versatile, and affordable. From trendy everyday outfits to rare Filipiniana pieces, enjoy premium fashion without the commitment or high cost.

  • Dress for the Moment

    Whether it's a wedding, photoshoot, or formal event, rent the perfect piece just when you need it. No storage worries, no last-minute shopping—just wear, enjoy, and return with ease.

  • A Mindful Choice

    Join a movement that values purpose over excess. By renting instead of buying new, you reduce fashion waste and support a more thoughtful, circular lifestyle.

  • Extend Your Garments’ Story

    Breathe new life into your collection. Let your pieces be part of meaningful moments for others—from rentals to everyday expression.

  • Grow with a Circular Community

    Join a movement that collaborates sustainably, boosts creativity, and shares value. Retasify connects you with changemakers who care about style, culture, and impact.
